Annotation. The  results  of  a study of plane-
parallel  filtration  in  a  fractured-porous  for-
mation developed by a rectilinear gallery with 
a perfect character and degree of opening un-
der the elastic-water-pressure regime are pre-
sented.

коллектор,  совершенная,  характер,  степень,  вскрытие,  упруго-
